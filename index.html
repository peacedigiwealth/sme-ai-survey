Informed Consent

Participant Consent Form

Please read the following carefully before proceeding.

1. Voluntary Participation
  • Your participation in this study is entirely voluntary.
  • You may withdraw at any time without penalty or consequence.
  • There are no right or wrong answers — only honest responses are needed.
2. Confidentiality & Anonymity
  • All responses are strictly anonymous and confidential.
  • No identifying personal information will be collected.
  • Data will be stored securely and accessible only to the research team.
3. Academic Use Only
  • This research is conducted solely for academic purposes.
  • Findings may be published in academic journals or dissertations.
  • Individual responses will never be disclosed or identified in any publication.
4. Research Purpose
  • Study Title: "AI Capability and Its Impact on Organizational Performance: Evidence from SMEs in Lagos State"
  • Data will be analyzed using SPSS statistical software.
